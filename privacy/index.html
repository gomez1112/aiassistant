Privacy Policy

Who We Are

This privacy policy applies to ai.assistant, which is operated by Transfinite LLC. It covers the app on iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.

What Data We Collect

Information users provide directly may include:

  • Files, documents, or user-generated content handled through the app
  • Prompts, uploads, or generated content used by AI-powered features

Information collected automatically through the app or related services may include:

  • Crash logs, diagnostics, and reliability data
  • Identifiers needed to recognize an account, device, session, or subscription state

Some data leaves the device to support app functionality such as accounts, syncing, purchases, support, or other online features.

How We Use Data

We use your data in a variety of ways, including:

  • To find bugs and keep the app stable.
  • To personalize the app experience for the user.
  • To manage billing-related access and entitlements.
  • To provide the AI feature the user chose to use.

When Data is Shared

When an app feature needs online processing, syncing, account handling, or support, relevant data may be sent off the device to operate that feature.

Payments and Subscriptions

The app offers in-app purchases or subscriptions through StoreKit.

Purchase or subscription information may be used to manage billing, entitlements, support, refunds, or account access.

Data Retention

Relevant data stays on the user’s device rather than being retained by the developer.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices and Rights

Some permissions are optional and are only used if the user chooses the feature that needs them.

Users can ask for deletion of their data using the contact or tools described below.

Where supported, users can ask for a copy or export of their data.

Where supported, users can request account closure or account-related deletion.

Privacy requests can be sent through https://transfinite.us/contact/.

Requests can be handled through Contact email.

AI Features

The app includes AI-powered features. When a user chooses to use those features, prompts, uploads, or related content may be sent to an AI provider to return a result.

The AI feature is part of core app functionality.

AI-related inputs or outputs may be retained according to the retention approach described in this policy.
